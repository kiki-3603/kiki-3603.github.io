ESA Research Fellow

Hello there. Ciarán here. I'm an astronomer working at the Space Telescope Science Institute in Baltimore as an ESA Research Fellow. My research focuses on the formation of stars, discs, and planets, and how the external environmental may influence these processes. I have a particular interest in stellar accretion. I observe protoplanetary discs in massive star forming regions with JWST to do this.

My Research

  • External Irradiation

    How does external UV and X-ray radiation change disc chemistry? How efficiently can these radiation fields destroy discs?. Is star formation itself influenced by external irradiation?

  • Steller Accretion

    A major fascination of mine is stellar accretion. I want to know if the accretion process can be influenced by environmental factors, or if it is driven entirely by internal processes.

  • Late-stage Infall

    Interstellar gas and dust falling back onto protoplanetary discs provides radical new pathways to disrupt the disc, alter its chemistry, and possibly even extend its life.
